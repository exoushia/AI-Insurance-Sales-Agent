SECTION 1: PRODUCT OVERVIEW
Clause 1.1 – "Swasthya Micro Bima" (SP005), UIN SWIHLIP24005V012024.
Clause 1.2 – Micro-insurance indemnity policy. Annual premium starting ₹799.
    Instant issuance via Aadhaar eKYC. No agent or paperwork required.
Clause 1.3 – Designed for India's uninsured lower-income population: gig
    workers, domestic help, small traders, rural households, daily-wage earners.
    Available in 11 Indian languages. Premium auto-debit via UPI mandate. Sum
    Insured: ₹1 Lakh or ₹2 Lakh. Simplified terms, minimal exclusions.

SECTION 2: DEFINITIONS
Clause 2.1 – Accident: Sudden, unforeseen, involuntary external event causing injury.
Clause 2.2 – Cashless Facility (कैशलेस): Company direct-pays empanelled hospitals.
Clause 2.3 – Claim (दावा): Formal demand for covered medical expense reimbursement.
Clause 2.4 – Co-payment (सह-भुगतान): Not applicable under this Policy.
Clause 2.5 – Deductible (कटौती): Not applicable.
Clause 2.6 – eKYC: Electronic Know Your Customer using Aadhaar-based biometric
    or OTP authentication, enabling instant Policy issuance without physical documents.
Clause 2.7 – Exclusion (बहिष्करण): Condition for which no benefit is payable.
Clause 2.8 – Family: Self + Spouse + up to 2 Children (91 days–18 years).
    Family Floater adds ₹300/year per additional member.
Clause 2.9 – Hospitalisation (अस्पताल में भर्ती): Admission for minimum 24
    consecutive hours in a registered hospital (including District Hospitals,
    Government hospitals, and AYUSH hospitals).
Clause 2.10 – Hospital: Any registered medical facility including District
    Hospitals, Community Health Centres, Government Medical Colleges, and
    IRDAI-compliant private hospitals. Minimum 5 beds acceptable for rural areas.
Clause 2.11 – Illness: Sickness requiring medical treatment and hospitalisation.
Clause 2.12 – Insured/Insured Person: Policyholder and covered family members.
Clause 2.13 – Micro-Insurance: Insurance product with Sum Insured up to ₹2 Lakh,
    regulated under IRDAI (Micro-Insurance) Regulations, 2005 as amended.
Clause 2.14 – Pre-Existing Disease (पहले से मौजूद बीमारी): Condition diagnosed or
    treated within 12 months prior to Policy inception (reduced waiting period
    per micro-insurance framework).
Clause 2.15 – Premium (प्रीमियम): Annual consideration starting ₹799 for SI ₹1L;
    ₹1,199 for SI ₹2L. Exclusive of GST at 18%.
Clause 2.16 – Sum Insured (बीमा राशि): ₹1,00,000 or ₹2,00,000 per Policy Year.
Clause 2.17 – UPI Auto-Debit: Automated premium deduction via UPI mandate
    from the Insured's bank account on the renewal date.
Clause 2.18 – Waiting Period (प्रतीक्षा अवधि): Reduced per micro-insurance framework.
Clause 2.19 – AYUSH Treatment: Treatment under Ayurveda, Yoga, Naturopathy,
    Unani, Siddha, Homeopathy at registered AYUSH facilities.
Clause 2.20 – Aadhaar eKYC: Aadhaar-based digital identity verification
    compliant with UIDAI guidelines and IRDAI eKYC regulations.

SECTION 4: COVERAGE AND BENEFITS
Clause 4.1 – In-patient Hospitalisation
    Room, nursing, boarding (no sub-limit); ICU (no sub-limit); surgeon and
    specialist fees; medicines and diagnostics; operation theatre charges.
    Government and District Hospitals accepted (including zero-cost public beds —
    claim restricted to actual charges).

Clause 4.2 – Pre-Hospitalisation: 30 days prior to admission.
Clause 4.3 – Post-Hospitalisation: 60 days from discharge.
Clause 4.4 – Day Care: 50 procedures listed in Annexure I (simplified list
    appropriate for common conditions: cataracts, dialysis, chemotherapy,
    minor surgeries). Day Care at Government hospitals accepted.
Clause 4.5 – AYUSH: In-patient AYUSH treatment at registered AYUSH hospitals.
    Covered up to 100% of SI. Inclusion of Government AYUSH hospitals.
Clause 4.6 – Ambulance: Road ambulance up to ₹1,000 per hospitalisation.
Clause 4.7 – Accident Cover: Accidental injuries covered from Day 1.
    Ambulatory accident treatment at hospital OPD (non-hospitalisation)
    covered up to ₹5,000 per event if hospitalisation not required.
Clause 4.8 – No Claim Bonus: 5% SI increase per claim-free year, maximum 25%.
Clause 4.9 – Restoration: Not available. (SI ₹1L/₹2L limits are final per year.)
Clause 4.10 – Newborn Coverage: Newborn automatically covered for 30 days post-
    birth under Family Floater. Full enrollment thereafter with separate premium.
Clause 4.11 – Government Scheme Coordination: If Insured is also covered under
    PM-JAY/Ayushman Bharat, Swasthya Micro Bima shall pay for expenses not
    covered or not reimbursed by PM-JAY, up to available SI.

SECTION 11: FAQs
Q1. How do I enroll without visiting an office?
A1. Download the Swasthya app, enter your Aadhaar number, verify via OTP, answer
    5 health questions, link your UPI ID, and receive your digital Policy in under
    5 minutes. No office visit, no documents needed at enrollment.

Q2. क्या सरकारी अस्पताल में इलाज का खर्च मिलेगा?
    (Will treatment expenses at Government hospitals be covered?)
A2. हाँ। सरकारी और जिला अस्पतालों में इलाज पूरी तरह कवर है। अगर वहाँ शुल्क
    शून्य है, तो आप दवाइयों, जाँच, और एम्बुलेंस जैसे खर्च का दावा कर सकते हैं।
    (Yes, Government and District Hospitals are fully covered. Where treatment
    is free, you can claim incidental expenses like medicines, diagnostics, ambulance.)

Q3. What is the PED waiting period?
A3. Only 12 months, compared to 48 months in standard policies. After just one
    year of continuous coverage, pre-existing conditions like diabetes and
    hypertension are covered for hospitalisation.

Q4. Can I pay premium daily via UPI?
A4. Yes. You can opt for daily micro-debit of ₹3/day (₹1L SI) or ₹4/day (₹2L SI)
    from your UPI-linked account. Annual total remains ₹1,095 (₹3/day) — slightly
    higher than the flat annual ₹799, with the convenience of micro-payments.

Q5. Does this policy work alongside PM-JAY Ayushman Bharat?
A5. Yes. If you are a PM-JAY beneficiary, Swasthya Micro Bima pays for expenses
    not covered by PM-JAY (such as medicines, diagnostics, or treatments in
    non-PM-JAY empanelled hospitals). The two policies complement each other.
