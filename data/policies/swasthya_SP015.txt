PRODUCT PHILOSOPHY
India has the world's largest diabetic population (101 million) and carries a disproportionate
burden of premature cardiovascular disease. Most health policies exclude or severely restrict
pre-existing cardiac and metabolic conditions. SP015 Heart + Diabetes Care is built specifically
for Indians who already have — or are at risk of — diabetes, hypertension, and heart disease.

This policy accepts PED cardiac and diabetic conditions after a 12-MONTH WAITING PERIOD
(versus the standard 36–48 months), provides comprehensive OPD coverage for chronic disease
management, and rewards measurable improvement in health outcomes with premium discounts.

CLAUSE 1: DEFINITIONS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1.1 DIABETES MELLITUS: Type 1 or Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus confirmed by:
    Fasting plasma glucose ≥ 126 mg/dL on two occasions; or HbA1c ≥ 6.5%; or
    Random glucose ≥ 200 mg/dL with symptoms. Includes pre-diabetes (HbA1c 5.7–6.4%).

1.2 CARDIOVASCULAR DISEASE (CVD): Coronary artery disease (CAD), history of myocardial
    infarction (MI), angina pectoris, cardiac failure, arrhythmias, valvular heart disease,
    cardiomyopathy, hypertensive heart disease.

1.3 HYPERTENSION: Blood pressure consistently ≥ 140/90 mmHg or ongoing antihypertensive
    medication at the time of application.

1.4 METABOLIC SYNDROME: Presence of 3 or more of: abdominal obesity (waist > 90 cm male /
    > 80 cm female), triglycerides ≥ 150 mg/dL, HDL < 40/50 mg/dL, BP ≥ 130/85, fasting
    glucose ≥ 100 mg/dL.

1.5 DIABETIC COMPLICATIONS: Conditions arising directly from diabetes — nephropathy,
    retinopathy, neuropathy, peripheral vascular disease, diabetic foot, diabetic ketoacidosis.

1.6 CHRONIC DISEASE MANAGEMENT PROGRAMME (CDMP): Swasthya's structured programme of
    care coordination, monitoring, and coaching for enrolled policyholders.

1.7 HEALTH MILESTONE: A quantifiable improvement in biometric targets (HbA1c, BP, BMI,
    lipid profile) that unlocks a premium discount at renewal.

CLAUSE 3: SCOPE OF IN-PATIENT COVER
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

3.1 IN-PATIENT HOSPITALISATION (from Day 1 of year 2 for PED conditions; Day 1 for
    acute non-PED conditions):
    a) Room, board, nursing (single-occupancy standard room — no daily cap from Year 2)
    b) ICU charges — no daily cap
    c) Surgical procedures: CABG, angioplasty, valve replacement, pacemaker insertion,
       cardiac ablation, renal transplant, dialysis initiation, amputation
    d) Diabetic emergencies: Diabetic ketoacidosis (DKA), hyperosmolar hyperglycaemic
       state (HHS), severe hypoglycaemia with hospitalisation
    e) Stroke — acute management
    f) Peripheral vascular disease — surgical interventions
    g) Diabetic foot — debridement, amputation, wound care procedures
    h) 541 IRDAI day-care procedures

3.2 PED WAITING PERIOD: 12 months from inception for pre-existing diabetes, hypertension,
    and cardiac conditions. After 12 months, ALL complications and hospitalisations arising
    from these conditions are fully covered.

3.3 PRE-HOSPITALISATION: 60 days (including specialist consults, pre-admission tests,
    medications ordered pre-surgery).
3.4 POST-HOSPITALISATION: 120 days (recognising extended cardiac and diabetic recovery).

CLAUSE 4: OPD AND CHRONIC DISEASE MANAGEMENT PROGRAMME (CDMP)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

4.1 OPD BENEFIT (included in plan, no sub-limit per visit):
    a) Diabetologist / Endocrinologist consultations
    b) Cardiologist consultations
    c) Ophthalmologist (diabetic retinopathy screening — annual fundus + OCT)
    d) Nephrologist (quarterly eGFR, urine albumin monitoring)
    e) Dietitian / Certified Diabetes Educator (6 sessions/year)
    f) Podiatrist (diabetic foot assessment — bi-annual)
    g) OPD prescriptions: Insulin (all types), oral hypoglycaemics, antihypertensives,
       statins, aspirin — covered within OPD benefit limit
    h) Diagnostic tests: HbA1c, fasting/PP glucose, lipid profile, renal function,
       urine microalbumin, ECG, echocardiography (2D echo), TMT (treadmill test)

4.2 CONSUMABLES COVERED (within OPD benefit):
    — Glucometer strips: up to ₹3,000/year (HDC-Silver), ₹5,000/year (Gold/Platinum)
    — Lancets and needles: up to ₹1,500/year
    — Continuous Glucose Monitor (CGM) sensors: up to ₹8,000/year (HDC-Gold and Platinum)
    — Blood pressure monitors: ₹2,500 one-time

4.3 CDMP — CHRONIC DISEASE MANAGEMENT PROGRAMME:
    All policyholders are enrolled in CDMP from Day 1. This is not an opt-in feature.

    CDMP Components:
    — Dedicated care coordinator (nurse/health coach) assigned to each policyholder
    — Digital health records integration with Swasthya app
    — Automated medication reminder and refill alerts
    — Quarterly biometric review (HbA1c, BP, weight, lipid profile)
    — Monthly telephonic check-in (HDC-Gold and Platinum)
    — Weekly wearable data review and coaching (HDC-Platinum only)
    — Annual comprehensive health assessment at empanelled centre (Medanta, Fortis,
      Max, Apollo, Narayana, or equivalent) — free of charge

4.4 TELEMEDICINE: Unlimited telemedicine consultations (GP + specialist) via Swasthya
    app — not charged against OPD benefit.

5.1 PREMIUM DISCOUNT UPON ACHIEVING HEALTH MILESTONES at annual renewal:

    Milestone Achieved (vs prior year)              | Premium Discount
    ------------------------------------------------|------------------
    HbA1c reduced by ≥ 1.0 percentage point        | 5%
    BP controlled (< 130/80 mmHg consistently)     | 5%
    BMI reduced by ≥ 2 points                       | 5%
    LDL cholesterol reduced by ≥ 20 mg/dL          | 3%
    All four milestones achieved simultaneously     | 20% (cap)

5.2 Milestones are verified by the CDMP care coordinator using lab reports uploaded
    to the Swasthya app. No third-party verification required for discounts < 10%.

5.3 CUMULATIVE CAP: Maximum total milestone discount: 20% per year.
5.4 REVERSAL: If health parameters worsen significantly, the discount is not applied;
    no penalty or premium increase beyond standard age loading.
