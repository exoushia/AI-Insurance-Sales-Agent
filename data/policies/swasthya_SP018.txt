PRODUCT PHILOSOPHY
SP018 AI Health Companion is Swasthya's flagship next-generation health insurance,
designed for health-conscious, digitally engaged individuals who want more than
passive financial protection. It integrates continuous health data from wearables,
AI-powered health coaching, predictive risk management, and genetic insights into
an insurance product that actively works to prevent illness — and rewards measurable
health improvement with premium discounts of up to 30%.

This is a COMPREHENSIVE INDEMNITY + BENEFIT HYBRID policy with coverage identical
to SP002 Protect Plus as the base layer — plus a suite of AI-powered and biomarker-
enabled features that have no equivalent in any other Swasthya product.

CLAUSE 2: AI HEALTH COMPANION PLATFORM

2.1 THE SWASTHYA AI ENGINE:
    Powered by SwasthyaAI™ (proprietary ML model trained on anonymised health data from
    2.4 million insured members). The AI engine:
    — Ingests wearable data (steps, sleep, HRV, resting heart rate, SpO2, ECG if available)
    — Cross-references with claims history and biometric data
    — Generates personalised health risk score (0–100, updated weekly)
    — Issues AI Health Insights — actionable nudges, alerts, and recommendations
    — Flags anomalies: sudden HRV decline, irregular sleep, SpO2 drop, resting HR spike

2.2 SWASTHYA AI COMPANION APP:
    — Health Score Dashboard: Weekly risk score, trend graph, percentile within peer group
    — AI Nudges: Daily personalised micro-interventions (sleep, nutrition, movement)
    — Connected wearables: Apple Watch, Fitbit, Garmin, Samsung Galaxy Watch,
      Mi Band, Whoop — automatic sync via HealthKit/Fit/API
    — Symptom Checker: NLP-powered symptom assessment (not a diagnostic tool;
      connects to telemedicine for any flagged risk)
    — Lab Results Upload: AI interprets lab reports and explains in plain language
    — Medication Tracker: Reminder, refill alerts, interaction checks

2.3 AI-ASSISTED CLAIMS:
    — Medical necessity pre-screening before hospitalisation (optional, speeds up pre-auth)
    — Auto-document extraction from discharge summaries using OCR + NLP
    — Estimated settlement timeline provided at claim initiation
    — AI fraud anomaly detection (operates in background; does not delay honest claims)

3.1 GENETIC TESTING BENEFIT:
    Upon enrolment (or within 12 months of first renewal), each SP018 policyholder
    receives ONE genomic health risk panel — free of charge.

    The genomic panel covers:
    — Cardiovascular risk (APOE, LPA, 9p21 loci)
    — Cancer predisposition (BRCA1/2, MLH1/MSH2/MSH6 for Lynch syndrome, APC)
    — Pharmacogenomics: Warfarin, clopidogrel, metformin, statins — tailors medication
      recommendations
    — Type 2 Diabetes predisposition (TCF7L2, PPARG, KCNJ11)
    — Thyroid risk markers

3.2 EXECUTION: Partner: MedGenome Labs or Strand Life Sciences (NABH-accredited).
    Saliva kit delivered at home. Results in 3–4 weeks via app.

3.3 DATA PROTECTION:
    — Genetic data stored in PDPB 2023-compliant encrypted vault.
    — Genetic data is NEVER used for claims adjudication or premium revision.
    — Insured may request deletion of genetic data at any time.
    — Results visible only to the Insured and their nominated clinician.

3.4 GENOMIC-INFORMED CARE PLAN: Based on results, SwasthyaAI generates a personalised
    5-year health roadmap — recommended screenings, lifestyle adjustments, and
    medication reviews to flag to the treating physician.
