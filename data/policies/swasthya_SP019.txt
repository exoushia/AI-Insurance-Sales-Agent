PRODUCT PHILOSOPHY
India's gig economy employs over 77 million workers — delivery riders, cab drivers,
domestic help, daily wage workers, construction labourers, freelancers, domestic
migrant workers — who are among the most exposed to health risk and the least covered
by formal insurance. SP019 Gig Worker Shield is built exclusively for this segment:

— ₹3/day premium (₹1L cover) — affordable at the lowest income level
— Aadhaar-only enrollment — no documents, no forms, no agent
— OPD covered from Day 1 — no waiting for outpatient treatment
— UPI/NACH auto-debit — seamless from any bank or payment app
— 11-language app + IVR — including regional languages
— Accident cover from Day 1 — the primary health risk for gig workers

Swasthya GWS is compliant with IRDAI (Micro-Insurance) Regulations, 2005 and the
Platform Economy Insurance Framework (IRDAI Discussion Paper, 2024).
