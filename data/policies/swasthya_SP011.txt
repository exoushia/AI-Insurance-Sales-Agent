PRODUCT PHILOSOPHY
This is a FIXED BENEFIT policy. It pays a pre-agreed daily cash amount for every day
of hospitalisation, regardless of the actual hospital bill. No bills. No receipts.
No haggling. The benefit is paid in addition to any other health insurance — it
compensates for income loss, attendant costs, travel, and incidentals that indemnity
policies do not cover.

1.1 DAILY CASH BENEFIT (दैनिक नकद लाभ): The fixed amount per day of hospitalisation
    chosen at inception, payable irrespective of actual medical expenses incurred.

1.2 ICU BENEFIT: 2× the Daily Cash Benefit for each day the Insured is in an Intensive
    Care Unit (ICU), High Dependency Unit (HDU), or Coronary Care Unit (CCU).

1.3 HOSPITALISATION DAY: Each 24-hour period (or part thereof exceeding 12 hours)
    of continuous in-patient stay. The day of admission AND day of discharge are each
    counted as separate hospitalisation days if the stay spans midnight.

1.4 CONVALESCENCE BENEFIT: An additional lump-sum payment for extended hospitalisation
    beyond 10 consecutive days.

1.5 QUALIFYING HOSPITALISATION: Admission to a registered allopathic or AYUSH hospital
    for a minimum of 24 hours for medically necessary treatment.

1.6 POLICY YEAR BENEFIT LIMIT: The maximum total daily cash payable in a policy year,
    expressed in days.

CLAUSE 4: SCOPE OF COVER AND BENEFIT CONDITIONS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

4.1 BENEFIT IS PAYABLE FOR:
    a) Any illness or injury requiring in-patient hospitalisation ≥ 24 hours
    b) Day-care procedures lasting ≥ 6 hours (50% of daily benefit, max 10 events/year)
    c) Government hospital admissions (no cashless requirement; discharge certificate sufficient)
    d) AYUSH in-patient treatment in registered hospitals

4.2 BENEFIT IS NOT PAYABLE FOR:
    a) OPD visits, emergency room attendance without admission
    b) Same-day discharge procedures under 6 hours
    c) Quarantine without active medical treatment (e.g., COVID isolation without symptoms)
    d) Cosmetic, dental (elective), or fertility treatment admissions
    e) Substance withdrawal/detox programmes (unless certified by psychiatrist)
    f) Naturopathy/wellness stays not under active medical supervision

4.3 MULTIPLE HOSPITALISATIONS: Benefits paid for each separate qualifying hospitalisation,
    subject to overall 60/90 day annual limit per plan.

4.4 SIMULTANEOUS CLAIMS: SP011 may be claimed simultaneously with SP001–SP010. This
    policy is explicitly designed as a complement to indemnity policies. The company
    shall not apply coordination of benefits or reduce this fixed benefit on account
    of amounts received under any other policy.
