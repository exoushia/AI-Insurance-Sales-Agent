PRODUCT OVERVIEW
Swasthya Corporate Care is a group health insurance master policy for mid-to-large
employers (50+ employees). It provides comprehensive, Day-1 PED cover, maternity benefits,
and mental health cover — no individual underwriting, no waiting periods for the standard
suite of conditions. Administered via the Swasthya HR Portal with real-time employee
enrolment, claims tracking, and utilisation dashboards.

समूह स्वास्थ्य बीमा (Samuh Swasthya Bima)।

1.1 MASTER POLICYHOLDER: The employer who enters into this Group Policy with Swasthya.
    Identified in the Schedule as the Proposer.

1.2 INSURED MEMBER: An employee of the Master Policyholder who is enrolled under this
    Group Policy. Includes confirmed permanent, contractual, and probationary employees
    (as specified in the Group Policy Schedule).

1.3 DEPENDANTS: Spouse (legally married; LGBTQ+ domestic partners accepted under
    notarised cohabitation affidavit); dependent children (up to age 25 / financially
    dependent); dependent parents (up to 2 sets — employee's and spouse's — optional).

1.4 FLOATER SI: The Sum Insured is shared among the employee and enrolled dependants
    in a policy year. In larger families (5+ members), an individual sub-limit per
    member may be specified in the Schedule.

1.5 ENDORSEMENT WINDOW: 30-day window each quarter during which the Master Policyholder
    may add or remove employees via the HR Portal.

1.6 MID-TERM ADDITION: New joinees may be added with immediate effect (no waiting
    period reset) within the endorsement window.

3.1 PRE-EXISTING DISEASE WAIVER — DAY 1:
    ALL pre-existing conditions are covered from Day 1 for all enrolled employees and
    their dependants. No individual medical examination. No waiting period for PED.
    This is a fundamental feature of group health insurance per IRDAI circular
    IRDAI/HLT/REG/CIR/140/06/2015.

3.2 MATERNITY BENEFIT — DAY 1:
    Maternity is covered from the first day of enrolment under the group policy.
    — Normal delivery: as per plan schedule (₹50K–₹2L)
    — C-section: 150% of normal delivery limit
    — Pre-natal OPD: ₹10,000 (all plans)
    — Post-natal OPD: ₹10,000 (all plans)
    — Newborn cover: From birth, Day 0, for 90 days (included in floater SI)
    — NICU: Up to ₹1,00,000 per birth event
    NOTE: No 9-month waiting for maternity. Available from enrolment date.

3.3 MENTAL HEALTH COVERAGE — DAY 1:
    In-patient and day-care psychiatric hospitalisation: Covered within SI, Day 1.
    OPD mental health: ₹10,000/year per employee (8 therapy sessions at ₹1,250 each).
    Telepsychiatry: Included in telemedicine benefit, not deducted from OPD limit.
    Compliance with Mental Healthcare Act, 2017 (Section 21 — equality of treatment).

3.4 COVID-19 AND EPIDEMIC COVER:
    Hospitalisation arising from notified epidemic/pandemic — covered within SI.
    Home quarantine treatment with positive RTPCR: ₹2,000/day × 14 days (fixed benefit).

3.5 CORPORATE WELLNESS PROGRAMME:
    — Annual health check-up: Comprehensive preventive package for all employees
      (CBC, lipid, HbA1c, thyroid, urine, ECG, X-ray, BMI assessment)
    — Mental health webinars: 12/year, delivered by Swasthya-empanelled psychologists
    — Ergonomics assessment: For WFH/hybrid employees — posture and workstation review

7.2 The departing employee is treated as having continuous cover for portability purposes.
7.3 Swasthya sends automatic portability offer letter to the employee's registered email
    upon HR-triggered offboarding.
