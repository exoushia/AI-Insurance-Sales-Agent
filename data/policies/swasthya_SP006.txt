SECTION 1: PRODUCT OVERVIEW
Clause 1.1 – "Swasthya Senior Shield" (SP006), UIN SWIHLIP24006V012024.
Clause 1.2 – Indemnity health insurance for senior citizens aged 60–80 years
    at entry. Individual and Senior Couple Floater basis. Lifelong renewability.
Clause 1.3 – Designed specifically for senior citizens and parents of existing
    policyholders. Features include age-graded co-payment; reduced PED waiting
    period (1 year per IRDAI 2024 guidelines for seniors); chronic disease
    management; domiciliary treatment; annual health check-up included.
    Sum Insured: ₹3L, ₹5L, ₹10L, ₹25L.

SECTION 2: DEFINITIONS
Clause 2.1 – Accident: Sudden, unforeseen external event causing bodily injury.
Clause 2.2 – Cashless Facility (कैशलेस): Direct payment to Network Hospital.
Clause 2.3 – Claim (दावा): Formal demand for covered medical expense payment.
Clause 2.4 – Co-payment (सह-भुगतान): Age-graded mandatory co-payment:
    10% for Insured Persons aged 60–69 years;
    20% for Insured Persons aged 70–80 years.
    Co-payment applies to all admissible claims. Does not reduce Sum Insured.
Clause 2.5 – Chronic Disease Management: Structured program for ongoing
    management of diabetes, hypertension, COPD, chronic kidney disease, and
    cardiovascular disease, including quarterly teleconsultations, medicine
    monitoring, and lab test reminders.
Clause 2.6 – Deductible (कटौती): Not applicable.
Clause 2.7 – Domiciliary Hospitalisation: Medical treatment at home for
    condition warranting hospitalisation, where patient cannot be moved.
Clause 2.8 – Exclusion (बहिष्करण): Conditions/expenses not covered per Section 7.
Clause 2.9 – Family: Insured + Spouse (for Senior Couple Floater). No children
    under this Policy; children should be insured under SP001/SP002.
Clause 2.10 – Hospitalisation (अस्पताल में भर्ती): Admission for ≥24 hours
    at registered hospital, except Day Care Treatments.
Clause 2.11 – Hospital: IRDAI-compliant registered hospital.
Clause 2.12 – Illness: Sickness or disease requiring medical treatment.
Clause 2.13 – Insured/Insured Person: Senior citizen Proposer and Spouse.
Clause 2.14 – Network Hospital: Company-empanelled hospital for cashless.
    12,000+ hospitals across India as of April 2024.
Clause 2.15 – Pre-Existing Disease (पहले से मौजूद बीमारी): Condition diagnosed
    or treated within 12 months prior to Policy inception (reduced per IRDAI
    Circular IRDAI/HLT/CIR/MISC/2024 on Senior Citizen Health Insurance).
Clause 2.16 – Premium (प्रीमियम): Annual consideration per Policy Schedule, excl. GST.
Clause 2.17 – Senior Couple Floater: Policy covering two senior citizens (spouses)
    with shared Sum Insured at discounted combined premium.
Clause 2.18 – Sum Insured (बीमा राशि): Max aggregate Company liability per Policy Year.
Clause 2.19 – Waiting Period (प्रतीक्षा अवधि): Duration during which certain
    conditions are not covered from Policy inception.
Clause 2.20 – Annual Health Check-up: Comprehensive preventive health examination
    covered once per Policy Year per Insured Person.

SECTION 3: ELIGIBILITY AND ENROLLMENT
Clause 3.1 – Entry Age: Minimum 60 years; Maximum 80 years for new enrollment.
    Lifelong renewability for existing policyholders beyond 80 years.
Clause 3.2 – Eligible Members: Individual (self) or Senior Couple Floater
    (Insured + Spouse). No dependent children under this Plan.
Clause 3.3 – Medical Underwriting: Mandatory medical examination for all applicants,
    irrespective of age or Sum Insured. Pre-acceptance tests at Company-empanelled
    diagnostics. Cost borne by Company if Policy accepted. Tests include:
    complete blood count, lipid profile, HbA1c, kidney function, ECG,
    chest X-ray, and doctor's physical examination report.
Clause 3.4 – Enrollment Documents: Proposal Form; Aadhaar/PAN/Passport; Age proof
    (birth certificate or school certificate or Aadhaar); Address proof; Medical
    examination reports; Existing health insurance details.
Clause 3.5 – Free-Look Period: 15 days from Policy receipt.

SECTION 4: COVERAGE AND BENEFITS
Clause 4.1 – In-patient Hospitalisation: Full indemnity for medically necessary
    treatment — room/boarding, ICU, surgeon, anaesthetist, specialist, OT,
    medicines, consumables, diagnostics, implants.

Clause 4.2 – Room Rent Sub-Limits:
    SI ₹3L: Room ₹3,500/day; ICU ₹7,000/day.
    SI ₹5L: Room ₹5,000/day; ICU ₹10,000/day.
    SI ₹10L: No sub-limit.
    SI ₹25L: No sub-limit.
    Proportionate deduction applies for room above entitled category.

Clause 4.3 – Pre-Hospitalisation: 60 days before hospitalisation.
Clause 4.4 – Post-Hospitalisation: 90 days from discharge.

Clause 4.5 – Reduced PED Waiting Period
    Per IRDAI's 2024 Circular on Senior Citizen Health Insurance, Pre-Existing
    Diseases are covered after only 1 year of continuous coverage under this Policy
    (reduced from standard 48 months). This is a landmark benefit for senior
    citizens who typically have multiple pre-existing conditions.

Clause 4.6 – Co-payment Structure
    Ages 60–69: 10% co-pay on all admissible claims.
    Ages 70–80: 20% co-pay on all admissible claims.
    Example (age 72, SI ₹5L): Admissible claim ₹1,20,000. Co-pay = ₹24,000.
    Company pays ₹96,000. Remaining SI: ₹3,80,000.

Clause 4.7 – Day Care Treatments: 570 procedures (Annexure I). Co-pay applies.
Clause 4.8 – Domiciliary Hospitalisation: Up to 25% of SI per Policy Year for
    conditions requiring hospitalisation but treated at home. Standard exclusions
    apply (asthma, hypertension, diabetes in domiciliary setting excluded).
Clause 4.9 – Annual Health Check-up: Full-body annual check-up at Company-
    empanelled diagnostics. Included at no cost. Package includes: CBC, blood sugar
    (fasting/PP), lipid profile, kidney function, liver function, thyroid function,
    urine analysis, chest X-ray, ECG, eye examination, dental screening, BMI.
Clause 4.10 – Chronic Disease Management Program (CDMP): Enrolled automatically
    upon diagnosis of diabetes, hypertension, COPD, CKD, or cardiovascular disease.
    Includes: quarterly teleconsultation with specialist nurse; medication adherence
    reminders; monthly HbA1c/BP tracking; annual specialist review; emergency
    helpline for symptom escalation.
Clause 4.11 – Ambulance: Road ambulance ₹3,000/hospitalisation. No air ambulance.
Clause 4.12 – AYUSH: In-patient AYUSH up to 50% of SI. Co-pay applies.
Clause 4.13 – Mental Health Coverage: In-patient psychiatric treatment up to
    ₹1,50,000/Policy Year. Co-pay applies.
Clause 4.14 – No Claim Bonus: 5% per claim-free year; max 25% of Base SI.
    NCB reduces by 5% upon any claim in a Policy Year.
Clause 4.15 – Organ Donor: Medical expenses for organ extraction from donor
    covered up to available SI.

SECTION 11: FAQs
Q1. Will my diabetes, hypertension, or heart condition be covered?
A1. Yes, after just 12 months of continuous coverage under Swasthya Senior Shield.
    The standard 48-month PED waiting period is reduced to 12 months specifically
    for senior citizen policies per IRDAI's 2024 guidelines.

Q2. मुझे 20% को-पेमेंट करना होगा — क्या यह बहुत ज़्यादा नहीं है?
    (I have to pay 20% co-payment — isn't that too much?)
A2. 70 वर्ष से अधिक उम्र के लिए 20% सह-भुगतान अधिक लग सकता है, लेकिन इसी
    कारण प्रीमियम अन्य योजनाओं की तुलना में कम है। साथ ही, आपका बेटा या
    बेटी (वयस्क बच्चे) इस प्रीमियम पर धारा 80D के तहत ₹50,000 तक की छूट
    पा सकते हैं। वास्तविक बोझ नेट टैक्स बेनिफिट के बाद काफी कम हो जाता है।
    (The 20% co-pay at 70+ keeps premium lower. Adult children paying the
    premium get up to ₹50,000 Section 80D deduction, significantly reducing
    the net cost.)

Q3. Is there any restriction on the number of hospitalisations per year?
A3. No restriction on number of hospitalisations. Claims are payable until
    the Sum Insured is exhausted for the Policy Year, subject to the 10% or
    20% co-payment on each claim.

Q4. What does the Chronic Disease Management Program include?
A4. CDMP is automatically enrolled for seniors with diabetes, hypertension,
    COPD, CKD, or cardiovascular disease. It includes: quarterly teleconsultation
    with a specialist nurse; personalised medication schedule; monthly vitals
    monitoring alerts; annual in-person specialist review; emergency escalation
    helpline for symptom deterioration. CDMP is included at no extra cost.

Q5. Can my son or daughter pay the premium on my behalf and claim 80D deduction?
A5. Yes. Adult children paying insurance premium for their senior citizen parents
    can claim a deduction up to ₹50,000 per year under Section 80D of the
    Income Tax Act, 1961, provided the parents are 60 years or older.
