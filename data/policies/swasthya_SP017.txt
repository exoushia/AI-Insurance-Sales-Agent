PRODUCT PHILOSOPHY
Small and medium enterprises (SMEs) are the backbone of India's economy — yet fewer
than 12% of SME employees have any employer-provided health insurance (ESIC threshold:
21+ employees; many SMEs operate below or outside ESIC). SP017 SME Suraksha brings
corporate-grade group health benefits to businesses with as few as 7 employees, with:
— Flat-rate premiums (no per-employee actuarial complexity)
— Zero waiting period for PED and maternity (group policy protection)
— Digital-only onboarding (no HR team needed — owner can manage the policy)
— UdyamCare empanelment: Swasthya is registered under the MSME Health Support Scheme

CLAUSE 1: ELIGIBILITY AND GROUP DEFINITION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1.1 ELIGIBLE ENTITIES: Any legally registered business entity with:
    — Minimum 7 employees (including proprietor/director where applicable)
    — Maximum 50 employees (above 50: refer to SP016 Corporate Care)
    — GST registration OR Udyam (MSME) registration OR Company/LLP registration

1.2 ELIGIBLE EMPLOYEES: All full-time, part-time, and contractual employees on payroll
    (as evidenced by salary slips or Form 16). Freelancers on direct contracts eligible
    if documented.

1.3 DEPENDANTS: Spouse + up to 2 dependent children (up to age 21).
    Parents: Available as add-on (Clause 8.2).

1.4 MINIMUM PARTICIPATION: 80% of eligible employees must be enrolled at inception.
    (Avoids adverse selection; waived for groups of 7–10 employees.)

1.5 BUSINESS TYPES SERVED: Retail shops, restaurants/food service, manufacturing
    (light), IT service firms, logistics/transport, healthcare clinics, coaching
    institutes, construction contractors, travel agencies, beauty/wellness salons,
    NGOs, startups (pre-revenue accepted with Udyam registration).

2.1 PLAN AND PREMIUM (per employee per year, floater — employee + spouse + 2 children)

    Plan         | SI per Family | OPD/employee | Premium/employee/yr
    -------------|---------------|--------------|---------------------
    SME-Basic    | ₹2 Lakhs     | ₹5,000       | ₹1,200
    SME-Standard | ₹3 Lakhs     | ₹8,000       | ₹1,800
    SME-Plus     | ₹5 Lakhs     | ₹12,000      | ₹2,800

    Illustration (15-employee retail shop, SME-Standard):
    Total annual premium: 15 × ₹1,800 = ₹27,000/year = ₹2,250/month
    (less than cost of one hospitalisation without insurance)

2.2 FLAT-RATE PRICING: Premiums above are FLAT — irrespective of the age or gender
    distribution of the group, for groups of 7–50 employees. The insurer pools
    the risk across Swasthya's SME book.
    Exception: High-hazard occupations (construction, mining, chemical plants) —
    +30% flat loading applied to the entire group.

2.3 GROUP SIZE BANDS AND DISCOUNT:
    7–15 employees   : Base flat rate
    16–30 employees  : 5% discount
    31–50 employees  : 10% discount

2.4 PREMIUM PAYMENT:
    — Annual: Full payment upfront (2% discount)
    — Semi-annual: No loading
    — Quarterly: +2% loading
    — Monthly via ECS/UPI auto-debit: +3% loading

2.5 RENEWAL EXPERIENCE LOADING:
    Loss Ratio (Claims/Premium)  | Renewal Premium Adjustment
    -----------------------------|----------------------------
    < 60%                        | −5% (good experience)
    60%–90%                      | No change
    91%–120%                     | +10%
    > 120%                       | +20% (maximum cap)
