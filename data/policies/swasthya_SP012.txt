PRODUCT PHILOSOPHY
Swasthya Personal Accident Shield is a fixed-benefit policy that provides financial
protection against the permanent and temporary consequences of accidental injury. It covers
accidental death, permanent total disability, permanent partial disability, and temporary
total disability — ensuring the Insured's family and livelihood are protected when accidents
disrupt earning capacity.

Hindi: यह "व्यक्तिगत दुर्घटना बीमा" (Vyaktigat Durghatna Bima) है जो दुर्घटना से होने वाली
क्षति की भरपाई करता है।

This policy covers ACCIDENTS ONLY. Illness, disease, and natural death are not covered.
An "Accident" means a sudden, unforeseen, and involuntary event caused by external,
visible, and violent means resulting in bodily injury.

CLAUSE 1: DEFINITIONS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1.1 CAPITAL SUM INSURED (CSI) / मूल बीमित राशि: The base amount chosen at inception,
    against which all benefit percentages are calculated.

1.2 ACCIDENTAL DEATH (आकस्मिक मृत्यु): Death occurring solely and directly as a result
    of an accident within 12 months of the accident date.

1.3 PERMANENT TOTAL DISABILITY (PTD / स्थायी पूर्ण विकलांगता): Irrecoverable loss of both
    hands, both feet, both eyes, one hand and one foot, one hand and one eye, or one foot
    and one eye; or complete and permanent loss of all cognitive function; occurring within
    12 months of the accident and certified by a specialist panel.

1.4 PERMANENT PARTIAL DISABILITY (PPD / स्थायी आंशिक विकलांगता): Irrecoverable loss of a
    single limb, digit, or sense organ, as per the Percentage Scale in Clause 3.3.

1.5 TEMPORARY TOTAL DISABILITY (TTD / अस्थायी पूर्ण विकलांगता): Total and temporary inability
    to engage in any occupation or perform duties of usual work for a period not exceeding
    104 weeks (2 years), resulting directly from accidental injury.

1.6 ACCIDENT DATE: The date and time of the accident as documented in the FIR, medical
    records, or employer's accident register.

3.1 ACCIDENTAL DEATH BENEFIT: 100% of CSI paid to nominated beneficiary.

3.2 PERMANENT TOTAL DISABILITY (PTD) BENEFIT:

    Type of Loss                                        | % of CSI
    ----------------------------------------------------|----------
    Loss of both hands, or amputation at/above wrists   | 100%
    Loss of both feet, or amputation at/above ankles    | 100%
    Loss of sight of both eyes                          | 100%
    Loss of one hand AND one foot                       | 100%
    Loss of one hand AND sight of one eye               | 100%
    Loss of one foot AND sight of one eye               | 100%
    Complete and irrecoverable loss of hearing in both  | 75%
    Loss of speech (permanent, certified by ENT)        | 75%
    Total and permanent paralysis of both limbs         | 100%

3.3 PERMANENT PARTIAL DISABILITY (PPD) BENEFIT SCALE:

    Body Part / Function Lost                           | % of CSI
    ----------------------------------------------------|----------
    Arm at shoulder joint                               | 70%
    Arm at elbow joint                                  | 65%
    Arm below elbow (including wrist)                   | 60%
    Thumb (entire)                                      | 25%
    Index finger                                        | 15%
    Middle, ring, or little finger (each)               | 10%
    Leg at hip joint                                    | 70%
    Leg at or above knee (thigh)                        | 60%
    Leg at or below knee                                | 55%
    Foot at ankle                                       | 40%
    Big toe                                             | 10%
    Other toes (each)                                   | 5%
    Sight of one eye                                    | 50%
    Hearing one ear                                     | 25%
    Loss of kneecap (patella)                           | 20%
    Vertebral injury causing paraplegia (partial)       | 50%

    Note: PPD benefit is paid as a percentage of CSI. Multiple PPD from one accident
    are cumulated but cannot exceed 100% of CSI in total. If PTD also claimed, PTD
    subsumes any simultaneous PPD claim.

3.4 TEMPORARY TOTAL DISABILITY (TTD) BENEFIT:
    Weekly benefit: 1% of CSI per week, maximum 104 weeks (2 years).
    Payment commences after a 7-day deductible period.
    Maximum weekly payout: ₹25,000/week (regardless of CSI).

    Illustration (CSI = ₹25 Lakhs):
      Weekly TTD benefit = 1% × ₹25L = ₹2,500/week
      If disabled for 8 weeks: (8 – 1 deductible) × ₹2,500 = ₹17,500

    Note: TTD ceases upon medical recovery, return to work, or 104 weeks, whichever first.
    Medical certificate from treating doctor required every 4 weeks during disability.
