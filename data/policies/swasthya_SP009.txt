SECTION 1: PRODUCT OVERVIEW
Clause 1.1 – "Swasthya Young Star" (SP009), UIN SWIHLIP24009V012024.
Clause 1.2 – Indemnity health insurance for young adults aged 18–35. Individual.
    Low premium (₹2,999/year at ₹3L SI). Sports injuries covered. Mental health.
    OPD for minor ailments. Telemedicine unlimited. Fitness rewards program.
Clause 1.3 – For students, first-time employees, and early-career individuals
    seeking comprehensive but affordable protection. Technology-first: app-based
    management, instant cashless, digital claims. Adventure sports rider available.

SECTION 2: DEFINITIONS
Clause 2.1 – Accident: Sudden, unforeseen external event causing bodily injury.
Clause 2.2 – Cashless (कैशलेस): Direct hospital bill settlement by Company.
Clause 2.3 – Claim (दावा): Formal demand for covered medical expense payment.
Clause 2.4 – Co-payment (सह-भुगतान): Not applicable.
Clause 2.5 – Adventure Sports: Activities including trekking above 5,000 metres,
    bungee jumping, paragliding, scuba diving, skydiving, white-water rafting,
    rock climbing, and motor/cycle racing. Covered via optional rider (Clause 4.14).
Clause 2.6 – Fitness Reward: Premium discount earned through verified physical
    activity tracking via the Swasthya app.
Clause 2.7 – Mental Health OPD: Outpatient psychiatric/psychological services.
Clause 2.8 – OPD Benefit: Outpatient consultation and treatment benefits.
Clause 2.9 – Sports Injury: Bodily injury sustained during participation in
    organised or recreational sports, including cricket, football, basketball,
    tennis, athletics, swimming, martial arts, yoga, and gym activities.
Clause 2.10 – Telemedicine: Online/video consultation with registered Medical
    Practitioner via the Swasthya app's integrated telemedicine platform.
Clause 2.11 – Hospitalisation (अस्पताल में भर्ती): ≥24 hours at registered hospital.
Clause 2.12 – Illness: Sickness or disease requiring medical treatment.
Clause 2.13 – Insured Person: Individual aged 18–35 years.
Clause 2.14 – Network Hospital: Empanelled cashless hospital; 12,000+ across India.
Clause 2.15 – Pre-Existing Disease (पहले से मौजूद बीमारी): Condition diagnosed or
    treated within 48 months prior to Policy inception.
Clause 2.16 – Premium (प्रीमियम): Annual consideration per Schedule, excl. GST.
Clause 2.17 – Sum Insured (बीमा राशि): ₹3L or ₹5L per Policy Year.
Clause 2.18 – Steps Goal: Daily step count tracked via smartphone or wearable,
    verified through the Swasthya app's activity tracking integration.
Clause 2.19 – Waiting Period (प्रतीक्षा अवधि): Period from inception during which
    certain conditions not covered.
Clause 2.20 – Wellness Score: Monthly score computed from verified fitness activity,
    sleep data, stress indicators, and preventive health actions in the app.

SECTION 3: ELIGIBILITY AND ENROLLMENT
Clause 3.1 – Entry Age: 18–35 years. Policy not renewable beyond 35 (must
    transition to SP001/SP002 by 35th birthday renewal).
Clause 3.2 – Eligible Insured: Individual only. Not family floater.
Clause 3.3 – Medical Underwriting: No medical exam required. 10-question health
    and lifestyle declaration. Accepted within 24 hours digitally.
Clause 3.4 – Enrollment: Entirely digital via Swasthya app or website.
    Aadhaar eKYC; selfie-based identity verification; UPI for payment.
    Policy issued digitally within 10 minutes.
Clause 3.5 – Free-Look Period: 15 days (cancel via app, full refund).

SECTION 4: COVERAGE AND BENEFITS
Clause 4.1 – In-patient Hospitalisation: Full indemnity — room (no sub-limit);
    ICU; surgeon; anaesthetist; medicines; consumables; diagnostics; implants.
Clause 4.2 – Pre-Hospitalisation: 60 days.
Clause 4.3 – Post-Hospitalisation: 90 days.
Clause 4.4 – Sports Injuries (Day 1 — No Waiting Period)
    Hospitalisation for injuries sustained during sporting activities (organised
    or recreational) covered from Day 1 at full Sum Insured. Includes:
    fractures, ligament tears, concussions, dislocations, muscle tears, cuts
    requiring hospitalisation, physiotherapy post-injury (up to ₹15,000).

Clause 4.5 – Mental Health Coverage
    In-patient: Up to ₹1,50,000 per Policy Year.
    OPD mental health: ₹10,000 per Policy Year (psychiatrist + psychologist
    + 6 therapy sessions + prescribed psychiatric medications).

Clause 4.6 – OPD Benefit (Minor Ailments)
    ₹5,000 per Policy Year (₹3L SI) or ₹8,000 (₹5L SI) for:
    doctor consultations for fever, cold, infections; pharmacy (prescription);
    basic diagnostics (blood test, X-ray). Maximum ₹1,000 per visit. 
    No hospitalisation required. Submit via app within 7 days.

Clause 4.7 – Unlimited Telemedicine
    Unlimited online/video consultations with general practitioners and
    specialists via Swasthya app's telemedicine platform. Available 24/7.
    Not deducted from OPD limit. Prescription issued digitally.
    Covers: GP consultations, second opinions, follow-up consultations,
    mental health consultations (telecounselling, up to 4/month).

Clause 4.8 – Day Care: 570 procedures (Annexure I).
Clause 4.9 – AYUSH: In-patient AYUSH up to 25% of SI.
Clause 4.10 – Ambulance: ₹2,000 per hospitalisation.

Clause 4.11 – Fitness Rewards Program
    Verified physical activity earns premium discount at next renewal:
    Average ≥7,500 steps/day (monthly): 5% premium discount at renewal.
    Average ≥10,000 steps/day (monthly): 10% premium discount at renewal.
    Maximum combined fitness discount: 10% (not cumulative with other discounts).
    Verification: GPS/accelerometer data from smartphone or paired wearable
    (Apple Watch, Garmin, Fitbit, Mi Band) synced to Swasthya app.
    Fitness discount does not apply to claim-year renewals.

Clause 4.12 – No Claim Bonus: 10% per claim-free year; max 50%.
    Note: Fitness discount and NCB are separate; both may apply if no claim.

Clause 4.13 – Restoration: 100% once per Policy Year for different illness.

Clause 4.14 – Adventure Sports Rider (Optional, SR-009)
    Available at additional premium of ₹1,500/year (₹3L SI) or ₹2,200/year
    (₹5L SI). Covers hospitalisation for injuries from:
    Bungee jumping; paragliding/paramotoring; scuba diving (recreational,
    ≤40 metres); skydiving/parachuting; trekking above 5,000 metres;
    white-water rafting (Grade IV/V); rock climbing; motor racing
    (amateur/track days). Professional/competitive sports covered up to
    sub-limit of 50% of SI. Excludes: illegal street racing, BASE jumping.

Clause 4.15 – Dental (Accidental Only)
    Dental treatment required due to Accidental Bodily Injury to natural
    teeth requiring hospitalisation: covered up to ₹15,000.

SECTION 11: FAQs
Q1. How does the 10,000 steps fitness discount work?
A1. Connect your phone's health app or wearable to the Swasthya app. The app
    tracks your daily steps throughout the year. If your monthly average is
    ≥10,000 steps/day (verified by app), you receive a 10% discount on your
    renewal premium. If average is 7,500–9,999 steps/day, you get 5%.
    The discount is calculated at renewal time.

Q2. क्या क्रिकेट या फुटबॉल खेलते समय चोट लगने पर कवर मिलेगा?
    (If I get injured playing cricket or football, am I covered?)
A2. हाँ। Swasthya Young Star में खेल-कूद के दौरान चोट Day 1 से कवर है, बिना
    किसी प्रतीक्षा अवधि के। इसमें फ्रैक्चर, लिगामेंट टियर, मांसपेशी की
    चोट आदि शामिल हैं। अस्पताल भर्ती की आवश्यकता होनी चाहिए।
    (Yes. Sports injuries are covered from Day 1 without waiting period —
    including fractures, ligament tears, muscle injuries — provided
    hospitalisation is required.)

Q3. What happens to my policy when I turn 35?
A3. Swasthya Young Star cannot be renewed after age 35. We will notify you 90 days
    before your 35th birthday. You transition automatically to Swasthya Protect
    (SP001) or Swasthya Protect Plus (SP002) at renewal. All your accumulated
    NCB credits and waiting period completions transfer to the new policy with
    no fresh PED waiting period.

Q4. Is telemedicine consultation really unlimited?
A4. Yes. Telemedicine consultations via the Swasthya app are unlimited and not
    counted against your OPD benefit. You can consult GPs, specialists, and
    mental health professionals (up to 4 telecounselling sessions/month).
    Available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Q5. I go trekking in the Himalayas regularly. Do I need the adventure sports rider?
A5. Standard trekking below 5,000 metres is covered as sports injury (Clause 4.4)
    without the rider. For treks above 5,000 metres (e.g., Stok Kangri, Mera
    Peak, EBC trek above Lobuche), the Adventure Sports Rider (SR-009) is
    required. The rider costs ₹1,500/year at ₹3L SI — highly recommended for
    regular high-altitude trekkers.

Q6. Does this cover gym injuries?
A6. Yes. Gym injuries (HIIT injuries, weight training accidents, treadmill
    falls, etc.) causing hospitalisation are covered from Day 1 as sports
    injuries under Clause 4.4. No adventure sports rider required for gym injuries.
